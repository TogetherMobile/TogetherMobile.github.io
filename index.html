How it works

Create riveting experiences that scale globally and seamlessly. Envision, build, and launch compelling engagements for people to share and enjoy their experiences.

Our platform produces same-day results with little or no integration. It’s backed by the power and hassle-free nature of our cloud-based approach. Plus you'll own the content for use anywhere—anytime—without being constrained by the terms and conditions associated with social media.

The platform offers:

Beautiful HTML5-based experiences that capture and display content

Modern cloud-based architecture that scales endlessly

Open architecture and a high-performance API that allows for integration into existing systems of record

Powerful SEO and social-linking capabilities to attract traffic from Google, Facebook, Twitter, and more

Seamless integration into new or existing mobile sites, desktop sites, and apps

Complete brand protection with robust content analysis and screening services that ensure content is displayed or routed as appropriate

Who we are

Rivet is an Austin-based startup founded and managed by an executive team with deep experience in content management, marketing, and ecommerce. We are taking user-generated content into its next generation by developing industry-changing solutions that tap into the genuine interests of your brand's customers, putting the customer’s experience back at the center of engagement.

Bio

Mike Svatek

CEO and Co-Founder
Bio

Mike Svatek

CEO and Co-Founder

Mike's ability to think big, as well as his passion and commitment to market evolving ideas and excellent products, led him to the principles upon which Rivet has been built: By working together, brands and consumers can make products, services, and experiences mind-blowingly better. Prior to co-founding Rivet, Mike was the Chief Strategy Officer and Chief Product Officer at Bazaarvoice, where he helped grow the business into the world's largest social SaaS company. He's also held executive and leadership roles at Baynote, a leading ecommerce personalization platform, and Interwoven, one of the world's largest and most successful content management software platforms. Mike earned his MBA from the University of California at Berkeley and his BBA in Management Information Systems from the University of Texas at Austin, where he currently serves on the Information Management Advisory Board for the McCombs School of Business.

Ben Lail

CTO and Co-Founder
Bio

Ben Lail

CTO and Co-Founder

Passionate about emerging technologies and building agile, innovative, and focused technology teams, Ben leads product, engineering, and operations teams at Rivet. His diverse career spans software development, information security, network operations, systems administration, and professional services. Recognized as an industry expert in security, he is a published author in the area of information security, and he is the architect of several large-scale consumer-facing systems. Prior to co-founding Rivet, Ben led the ethical hacking team for the State of Texas. Tasked with assessing the security of data centers, agencies, and public universities for the State of Texas, his team helped to ensure the security of the systems that operate one of the world's largest economies. Ben was a senior systems engineer for the BSAFE SDK division of RSA Security, where he was awarded the distinction of being named Systems Engineer of the Year, and has also held the positions with Advanced Micro Devices, Dell, Resilience, Array Networks, and Accudata Systems. He attended the University of Texas at Austin.

Dianna Koltz

Bio

Dianna Koltz

VP of Sales and Services

Having been publicly acknowledged by Direct Marketing News as a "30 under 30" for her ability to lead and transform growing teams, Dianna is fiercely committed to providing measurable results for clients. Before joining Rivet, Dianna held the role of VP of Client Services at Marketvine by Dell, where she was awarded the Dell Gold Award for her leadership. Ben Schneider

VP of Product
Bio

Ben Schneider

VP of Product

Engaging and connecting consumers through technology has been the main focus of Ben’s tenure in product and business management. Prior to joining Rivet, Ben oversaw the strategic and operational direction of social products as Vice President and General Manager at Demand Media, where he managed two key product lines: Pluck, an integrated community platform, and CoveritLive, a leading real-time social content solution. Under his leadership, from 2011 to 2014 CoveritLive grew from a recent acquisition that had been positioned as a free product to a multi-million dollar product line that went on to be re-acquired. Ben held roles leading product for various businesses over his time with Pluck and Demand Media, and formerly held technology and product leadership positions at Affiniscape, which is now YourMembership. Ben earned his BBA in Management Information Systems from the University of Texas at Austin and has served as President, Vice President, and a Board Member for The University of Texas Longhorn Alumni Band.
